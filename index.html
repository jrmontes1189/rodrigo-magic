About the Magician

A Life Devoted
to the Impossible

With over twenty years dedicated to the art of magic, my journey began with a simple trick that sparked a lifelong obsession. What started in Guatemala became a passion that took me from practicing one trick a day to performing on national television and entertaining audiences across the United States.

I've had the privilege of starring in Guatemala's first national magic TV show, performing for live audiences, and creating unforgettable moments at private events, corporate shows, and intimate gatherings, from Guatemala to Los Angeles.

My style blends close-up sleight of hand, coin magic, mentalism, and interactive magic that happens right in the spectator's hands. I don't just perform magic, I create experiences, moments of wonder, and memories people talk about long after the show is over.

Because to me, magic isn't about tricks. It's about how people feel when they see the impossible happen right in front of them.
