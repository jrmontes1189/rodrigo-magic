Frequently Asked Questions

♠ ♣
How far in advance should I book? +

For most events I recommend booking at least 3–6 weeks in advance. Peak seasons (holidays, spring/summer) fill up quickly, so earlier is always better. That said, I do accommodate last-minute requests when my calendar allows.

Do you travel outside Los Angeles? +

Absolutely. While Los Angeles is my home base, I perform throughout California and take national engagements regularly. Travel fees may apply for events outside the greater LA area. International bookings are also considered for the right opportunity.

What is included in a typical performance? +

Each booking is tailored to your event. A typical corporate show includes pre-show consultation, a 45–60 minute stage performance, walkaround mingling entertainment, and professional setup/breakdown. Private party packages focus on close-up magic and mentalism.

What are your technical requirements? +

Requirements vary by show type. Close-up and parlor shows need minimal setup. Stage productions may require a stage space, basic lighting, and sound. I provide a detailed technical rider upon booking confirmation and work flexibly with your venue.

Is a deposit required to secure a booking? +

Yes, a 30% deposit is required to reserve your date, with the balance due two weeks prior to the event. I accept all major credit cards, bank transfer, and PayPal. All bookings include a written contract for your protection.

Do you perform for children's parties? +

My primary focus is adult and all-ages audiences for corporate, private, and wedding events. I do offer select family-friendly shows — please mention this in your inquiry and I'll advise on the best format for your audience mix.
